About Us

At Fomy, we're passionate about creating delicious, high-quality ice cream that everyone can enjoy. Whether you're in the mood for a creamy, milk-based soft serve or a refreshing water-based option, we've got you covered. We don't just stop at the ice cream itself. We also carefully curate all of our toppings to make sure they pair perfectly with our ice creams. We believe that a great topping can take our delicious ice cream to the next level, and that's where we offer a wide range of homemade fruit compotes, sauces, and other delicious options to choose from. Whether you prefer a sweet and fruity topping or something rich and indulgent, we've got a topping for every taste.
